VSim Help and Hot Key Menu

Narratives

Open, close, and scale the Narrative bar by dragging its lower edge
Click + in the Narrative bar to begin building a Narrative
Double click or on Narrative title or select and click Open to review Narrative Nodes
Click p on the keyboard when a Narrative is selected to toggle between play/pause

Embedded Resources (ERs)

Open, close, and scale the ER bar by dragging its upper edge
Click + in the to add a new ER
Toggle display between Show All and Show Local/Global
Turn on/off Resource Legend through Filter
Right clicking anywhere clears the displayed ERs
